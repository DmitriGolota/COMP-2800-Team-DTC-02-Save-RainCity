About Us

We are a group of 5 Junior Developers from BCIT. Our members are Dmitri Golota, Brian Romaniuk, Joshua Camacho, Maximilian Wu, and Ralph Li. We are passionate about bettering our community so, we developed this app to be able to bring awareness to the Climate Emergency Action Plan set forth by the city of Vancouver.
