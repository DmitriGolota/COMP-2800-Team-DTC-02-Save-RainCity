About Us

We are a group of 5 Junior Developers from BCIT. Our members are Dimitri Golota, Brian Romaniuk, Joshua Camacho, Maximilian Wu, and Ralph Li. Passionate about bettering our community we developed this app to hopefully be able to bring awareness to the Climate Emergency Plan.

Meet the Devs

Dmitri Golota

Dmitri Golota is one of the developers that worked on Save RainCity. As an aspiring Game Dev he has created most of the art and sound assets that you might come across inside the game. He has helped his team manuevre through planning, design and game mechanic implementation throughout the project while keeping the entire team motivated.

Brian Romaniuk

Being an avid gamer and aspiring software developer, Brian Romaniuk is passionately involved in the Save RainCity project. As one of the developers for Save RainCity, Brian worked extensively on the game ideation, planning, design, CEAP research, and back-end implementation.

Joshua Camacho

Joshua Camacho is a novice developer who is passionate about gaming, creativity, and the environment. Joshua and the team worked together closely to bring their vision to life.
